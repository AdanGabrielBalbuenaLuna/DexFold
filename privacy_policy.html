Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DexFold ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Android application.

Information We Collect

DexFold does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal data.

Third-Party Services

DexFold retrieves Pokémon data from the PokéAPI (pokeapi.co), a free and open public API. No personal data is sent to this service — only requests for Pokémon information. Please refer to PokéAPI's own privacy policy for more details about how they handle requests.

Internet Access

The app requires an internet connection to fetch Pokémon data from the PokéAPI. This connection is used solely to retrieve publicly available Pokémon information and is not used to collect or transmit any user data.

Children's Privacy

DexFold does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
